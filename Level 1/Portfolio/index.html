About Me

Introduction

My academic path has been one of steady growth and determination — completing my 10th grade with 75% during the challenging COVID batch, followed by securing over 90% in my 12th under the Uttarakhand Board (UBSE).

This mindset has helped me navigate my education with a focus on both understanding and applying knowledge.

Beyond Academics

Outside the academic sphere, my curiosity has always been my strongest trait. My name, Harshit Pathak, literally translates to “Cheerful Scholar” — and in many ways, it perfectly reflects who I am. From a young age, I was fascinated by science — especially astronomy, cosmology, and the mysteries of the universe. Equally, I was drawn to computers and anything that worked with logic.
As a child, I wasn’t satisfied with just playing with toys; I wanted to understand how they worked. Building blocks intrigued me, but so did dismantling motor cars or toy guns just to see the mechanisms inside. That same hands-on curiosity also fuels my passion for gaming and game development. I’ve always loved exploring immersive virtual worlds, but even more, I enjoy learning how those worlds are built — the art, the code, and the logic that make them come alive.
This mix of wonder for the universe, fascination for technology, and creativity in gaming has shaped how I approach learning and problem-solving — with a blend of imagination, analysis, and the drive to explore what lies beneath the surface.
